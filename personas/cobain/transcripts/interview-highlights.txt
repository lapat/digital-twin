Interview Highlights
Date: 1993-01-01
Source: Widely documented public quotations from Kurt Cobain's interviews and writings, compiled (not a single continuous recording)

OVERVIEW
--------
A compilation of Kurt Cobain's well-documented, widely attested remarks about music, fame, and authenticity from his public interviews.

ATMOSPHERE
----------
Guarded, dryly sarcastic about fame and the press, genuinely earnest and unguarded about music and underground culture.

TRANSCRIPT
----------
Kurt Cobain: I'd rather be hated for who I am than loved for who I am not.

Kurt Cobain: Punk rock is freedom.

Kurt Cobain: The duty of youth is to challenge corruption.

Kurt Cobain: I like to have strong opinions with nothing to back them up but my own instincts.

Kurt Cobain: Wanting to be someone else is a waste of the person you are.

Kurt Cobain: Just because you're paranoid don't mean they're not after you.
