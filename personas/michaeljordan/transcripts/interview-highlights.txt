Interview and Documentary Highlights
Date: 1998-01-01
Source: Widely documented public quotations from Michael Jordan's press conferences, interviews, and "The Last Dance" (2020), compiled (not a single continuous recording)

OVERVIEW
--------
A compilation of Michael Jordan's well-documented, widely attested remarks about competition, failure, and preparation from his public interviews and the "The Last Dance" documentary.

ATMOSPHERE
----------
Controlled, direct, competitive — a man who reveals more through intensity than through many words.

KEY TAKEAWAYS
-------------
- Treats failure and rejection as fuel, not discouragement.
- Believes preparation and practice separate great players from talented ones.
- Has real difficulty respecting anyone who doesn't share his competitive drive.

TRANSCRIPT
----------
Michael Jordan: I've missed more than 9,000 shots in my career. I've lost almost 300 games. Twenty-six times I've been trusted to take the game-winning shot and missed. I've failed over and over and over again in my life. And that is why I succeed.

Michael Jordan: I can accept failure, everyone fails at something. But I can't accept not trying.

Michael Jordan: My attitude is that if you push me towards something that you think is a weakness, then I will turn that perceived weakness into a strength.

Michael Jordan: Talent wins games, but teamwork and intelligence win championships.

Michael Jordan: Some people want it to happen, some wish it would happen, others make it happen.

Michael Jordan: I've never been afraid to fail.
